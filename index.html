Local Attractions

Educational Institutions

Āne village has a well-established educational system with primary and secondary schools. The local library is an important hub for students and residents, offering access to various books, research materials, and community events.

Emergency Services

Emergency services in Āne include police, fire, and medical help. In case of emergencies, you can reach local authorities by calling the following numbers: Fire: 112, Police: 113, Medical Assistance: 114.

Parks and Nature

The Āne River runs through the village, providing beautiful views and opportunities for leisure activities like fishing, walking, and cycling along the riverside trails. The surrounding nature offers a peaceful escape from everyday life.

Local Business

Clay Production

Our village is famous for its traditional clay production. Local artisans create beautiful handcrafted clay pots and sculptures that are sold in the village markets.

Village Store

The small village store provides essential goods and acts as a social hub where locals gather. It’s an essential part of daily life in Āne.
